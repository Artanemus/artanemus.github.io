Why use SwimClubMeet?

Your local swimming club differs from a large competitive event in many ways.

Typically a local swim club look like this ...

Nominations for events happens at the pool gate. Parents are keen to get the event's started so they can get the kids home and to bed, but the night start's slowly, there's a cue to nominate.

Most club's have small membership numbers, say 50-80 swimmers, so it's normal to mix gender and ages into individual heats.

Parents are given the jobs of timekeeper or marshal. And someone is sitting in a corner, with a laptop, setting the meet up, probably into a spread sheet. There's lots of paper being couriered around the pool.

What's needed ...

Quick, simple way to get members nominated.
A single button to build heats.
Easy sheet production for marshal and timekeepers.
Or better still - remove paper and ink.
Store and archive race history and some basic club member details.
Tabulate a report of the night's session.

SCM's Mantra.

SCM is focused on 'what is needed' and most importantly, keep it simple.

Just getting started ....

All you need to get started is a lap-top and printer.

SwimClubMeet is the core app - it does it all. It creates sessions and events, nominate and Auto-Builds all your heats, enter race-times. It also manages members details. It does reporting.

It'll print-out all the forms you need. Such as the Marshal's rally sheet and Timekeeper's entry sheet. At the end of the night you can create a session report for distribution to parent and friends.

If you have the resources ...

Have you got a Wi-Fi, more than one computer?

With two laptops and a router, get connected then share the database server and run SwimClubMeet on both. Nominate swimmers twice as fast.

Go one step further ...

Then there's the support applications.

SCM gives you a simple kiosk type app for members to Self-Nominate at the entry gate.

You may wish to use the Marshal and Timekeeper apps for a paperless club night.

Note: The above applications are only for MS Surface PRO or simular. Other platforms will be available in the future.

Currently in development

A stand-alone app just to manage membership data, should you wish to delegate tasks.

A leader-board app to track swimmer and house scores, in real time. This isfor you club's end-of-season finals and will also be used by the carnival application.

Future ideas and plans

A stats package. Lots of charts and stats, for the annual committee meeting.

A carnival application to make it easy for neighboring clubs to challenge each other to a 'swim-off'.

A report package for some additional forms, for example, bar-coded membership cards, accreditation and podium certificates, etc. A preNominate app that uses email and PDF data-forms. Fully automated. It avoids using web services.

All developement of SCM is being done on a cross-platform compiler. This means that Android and iOS versions of nominate, marshal and timekeeper application will eventually be delivered.
